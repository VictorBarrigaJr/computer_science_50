About Auditory

Auditory is a music discovery engine that provides, free, on the spot, relevant music recommendations based on a search query.

Our mission is to help people easily learn about music they might like.

For most recommended items, additional details are available once the user decides to click on that particular item. These additional details may include a description, a video clip, and links towards helpful resources that cn help users form a better impression on their recent discovery.
